WELCOME TO GROW

Started in 2008, Grow is a small charity working with communities to create community gardens in their area. It's as much about growing community as it is about growing our food and flowers!

We have two gardens at preset; one in the Waterworks Park in North
Belfats and one in Helens Bay on Helen's Bay Organic Farm.
